  ## About

  I work where architectural practice, BIM workflows, and programming overlap.

  My background is in architectural engineering and BIM management, with experience ranging from detailed hospital and airport work in Sweden to digital twin and indoor
  navigation systems in Japan. I am especially interested in building pipelines that preserve architectural meaning while making spatial data usable in downstream systems.

  ---

  ## Current Focus

  - BIM workflows and model intelligence
  - Digital twins and indoor navigation
  - Geospatial data transformation
  - Agentic and AI-assisted automation
  - Tooling that reduces manual conversion steps between AEC and interactive systems

  ---

  ## Selected Work

  ### Shinjuku Indoor Navigation
  Detailed Revit station interiors translated into Unity and connected with PLATEAU's city-scale digital twin for seamless indoor-outdoor navigation.

  [View project](https://malmqvist.dev/en/projects/shinjuku-nav)

  ### Ersta Sjukhus
  Award-winning hospital project work including BIM reconstruction, facade modeling, and interdisciplinary coordination.

  [View project](https://malmqvist.dev/en/projects/ersta-sjukhus)

  ### Revit to GeoPackage Plugin
  A native Revit export workflow that removes legacy multi-step conversions and preserves more useful BIM data for geospatial use.

  [View project](https://malmqvist.dev/en/projects/revit-geopackage)
